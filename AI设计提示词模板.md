# 给 AI 设计工具的提示词模板

把项目 UI 介绍.md 里的内容结合下面的模板使用：

## Midjourney / DALL-E / 通义万相 通用模板

```
A mobile app UI screenshot, realistic mockup on a phone screen.

Style: pink aesthetic, cute dessert theme, round cards, soft shadows, minimal Chinese food diary app.

[HERE写具体页面描述]

Color palette: background #FFF0F0, cards #FFF5F5, accent pink #FADADD, primary text #3C2415, secondary text #A0806E, borders #F0D0D0.

Round corners (12px cards, 24px buttons), iPhone 15 Pro frame, portrait orientation.
```

## 给 Midjourney 的页面对照表

| 你要设计 | 提示词里写 |
|---------|-----------|
| 首页 | A scrollable feed of dessert cards with photos, ratings, and shop names. Top nav bar with cake emoji title. Search bar with AI icon. Bottom tab bar with 2 tabs. Floating dice button at bottom right. |
| 详情页 | A dessert detail screen showing a full-width photo, spoon ratings, flavor tags as pink pills, shop name, price, and three action buttons at the bottom. |
| 记录页 | A multi-step dessert recording form. Step shows: photo taking area, flavor selection grid (matcha, cocoa, cheese etc.), sweetness selector, and note input. |
| 统计页 | A stats dashboard with a weekly summary card showing bar chart, shop ranking list with medals, rating distribution bars, and an AI report card. |
| 周报页 | A weekly report screen with an AI summary card on gradient background, three stat cards, daily bar chart, flavor ranking bars, and shop ranking. |
